## 1. Pipeline SLAs

|--------|--------|

## 2. Escalation Procedures

### Severity P1 — Critical (SLA breach imminent or breached)
- **Response time:** 15 minutes
- **Channel:** PagerDuty alert → on-call engineer → Slack #incidents
- **Action:** Immediate investigation, stakeholder notification within 30 min

### Severity P2 — High (pipeline delayed > 50% of SLA window)
- **Response time:** 30 minutes
- **Channel:** Slack #data-alerts + team channel
- **Action:** Investigation within 30 min, status update every 30 min

### Severity P3 — Medium (pipeline delayed but within SLA)
- **Response time:** 2 hours
- **Channel:** Slack team channel
- **Action:** Investigation within 2h, resolved in same business day

### Severity P4 — Low (informational, no breach)
- **Response time:** Next business day
- **Channel:** Jira ticket
- **Action:** Scheduled fix in next sprint
